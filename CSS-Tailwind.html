Exercise 1 - Typography & Spacing

Recipe

Potatoes gratin 

                INGREDIENTS
                ~800 g ready-made potato gratin (serves 2)
                150 g bacon pieces (or any preferred substitute)
                Mixed frozen vegetables (or fresh, any kind you like)
                Grated cheese
                Oven-safe baking dish

                PROCESS
                Preheat oven to 220°C.

                Add mixed frozen vegetables to the baking dish.
                Spread the bacon pieces evenly on top.
                Add the potato gratin over the bacon and vegetables.
                Finish with a generous layer of grated cheese.

                Bake for about 40 minutes,
                or until the top is golden and the gratin is bubbling.

                OUTPUT
                A simple, comforting oven dish that requires almost no effort,
                just layer everything in a dish and let the oven do the work.
